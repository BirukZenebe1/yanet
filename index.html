Beauty Tips & Tricks

Bite-sized wisdom to level up your beauty routine instantly.

01

Always Apply SPF

Sunscreen is the single best anti-aging product. Use SPF 30+ daily, even on cloudy days and indoors near windows.

02

Hydrate Inside Out

Drink water throughout the day and layer a hyaluronic acid serum under moisturizer for plump, dewy skin.

03

Less Is More

A focused routine of 4-5 great products beats a 12-step regimen. Give each product time to work before adding more.

04

Double Cleanse at Night

Use an oil cleanser first to dissolve makeup and sunscreen, then follow with a gentle water-based cleanser.

05

Pat, Don't Rub

Gently pat serums and creams into your skin instead of rubbing. It reduces irritation and boosts absorption.

06

Clean Your Brushes

Wash makeup brushes weekly to prevent bacteria buildup that causes breakouts and skin irritation.
