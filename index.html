Daily Wisdom

Beauty Rituals

♦

Sunscreen is the single best anti-aging product. Use SPF 30+ daily, even on cloudy days and indoors near windows. Reapply every 2 hours for maximum protection.

Layer hyaluronic acid under moisturizer for dewy skin. Drink water throughout the day — aim for at least eight glasses daily.

A focused routine of four or five great products beats a twelve-step regimen. Give each product time to work before adding more.

Start with an oil cleanser to dissolve makeup and sunscreen, then follow with a gentle water-based cleanser for a truly clean canvas.

Gently pat serums and creams into your skin. It minimizes irritation and maximizes absorption for noticeably better results.
